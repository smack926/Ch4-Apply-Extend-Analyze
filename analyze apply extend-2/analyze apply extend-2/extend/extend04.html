Hexadecimal, RGB, and HSL color codes each have their advantages. Hexadecimal is concise and convenient, RGB offers precise control over color components, and HSL is easier to understand and manipulate.

Preferred color code format: I prefer HSL because it's easier to manipulate hue, saturation, and lightness to achieve the desired effect.

HWB (Hue, Whiteness, Blackness) is similar to HSL but represents colors differently, especially when dealing with shades, tints, and tones.

CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Key/Black) is used in printing and ensures color accuracy on paper by specifying ink percentages.
